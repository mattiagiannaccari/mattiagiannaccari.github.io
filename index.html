About me

I am deeply passionate about computer science, which is at the core of my academic and professional journey. I thrive on solving complex problems and exploring innovative solutions, combining methodical thinking with creativity. I like it when my work piques my curiosity and I continuously seek to expand my knowledge and skills in this ever-evolving field.

Alongside my dedication to computer science, music plays a central role in my life. I am a classical pianist and opera singer, and I find great joy and inspiration in performing and studying classical music. Music fuels my creativity and offers a unique balance to my technical pursuits.

Beyond my primary passions, I am fascinated by architecture, design, and physics. I enjoy spending my free time taking photographs and developing them, studying new languages, and discovering how things work. Whether through coding, composing, or capturing moments through a camera lens, I am always eager to learn and create.
